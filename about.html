About Me

Hello my name is Kenny Andre Holmen, I am an Front end developer. How I Ended up in the field is a bit of a coincidence. While I was taking my degree in comparative politics I tried a introduction to programming course which opened my eyes to what I wanted to do with my life.

After finishing the degree I enrolled in Noroffs front end program. Where I learned everything from UI/UX design and prototyping, to setting up headless CMS with Strapi and WordPress. I have learned to code responsive and accessible websites that gives a good user experience.
